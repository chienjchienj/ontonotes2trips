# stimulate
## 1.1: excite to growth or (greater) activity, arouse

  *  A good teacher will stimulate the students' imaginations.
  *  Smoke stimulates the germination of many western Australian plants.
  *  Don't drink coffee after 3:00p.m. It will stimulate you too much.
  *  Rad54 protein stimulates the postsynaptic phase of Rad51 protein.
