disclose

1.1: reveal something previously secret

  • The auction house would not disclose the price at which the van Gogh had sold.
  • The actress would not disclose her age.
  • Detainees' names must be disclosed under federal FOI Act.
  • Claims that arise during or after the bankruptcy petition is filed do not have to be disclosed.

1.2: expose by removing a cover

  • The curtain rose to disclose a stunning set.
  • The outer coffin was lifted to disclose an inner coffin made of gold.
  • The vapors lifted and disclosed the mountains from which they had taken their first departure.
