demand

1.1: forcefully request

  • The victim's family is demanding compensation.
  • The boss demanded that he be fired immediately.
  • The bank demanded payment of the loan.
  • I demand an explanation.
  • Record and film companies have demanded access to internet logs.
  • He demanded that the teaching of Dante be suspended in Italian schools in immigrant areas.

1.2: necessitate, require

  • This job demands a lot of personal sacrifice.
  • This situation demands the promotion of collaborative culture.
  • This position demands an uncommon balance of creativity and drive.
  • The situation imperatively demands that all should contribute full measure of devotion.
