plan

1.1: intend, mean

  • He plans to be in graduate school next year.
  • Let's plan to meet at six.
  • We never planned for this to happen.

1.2: devise, design

  • Do you think they are planning an attack before the election?
  • They have spent weeks planning the new marketing campaign.
  • The organizers have planned the parade route to run through the city center.
  • He didn't plan wisely for his retirement.
