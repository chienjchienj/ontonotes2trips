plant

1.1: place into the ground for growing

  • I planted 3 kinds of peppers this year.
  • The professor planted a thought in her mind.

1.2: place firmly

  • He planted a knee in the back of his opponent.
  • Plant a kiss right here.
  • They planted their flag at the top of the hill.

1.3: place secretly, often for later discovery

  • Kurdish rebels say they planted the Turkish resort bomb.
  • Then the Mossad planted the misinformation with Arab intelligence agencies.

1.4: establish, settle

  • The Spanish came shortly after the Dutch; they planted themselves in the north around Tamsui and Keelung.
  • Every fall, they plant fish in the lake.
