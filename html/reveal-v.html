reveal

1.1: uncover, make visible, show

  • He pulled the cloth away to reveal a rabbit in his hat.
  • The inspection revealed that the fuselage had a large crack.
  • The twinkle in her eye revealed a sense of humor.

1.2: disclose, make known

  • The actess won't reveal how old she is.
  • The names of the victims were revealed after the families were contacted.
  • The collector will not reveal what he paid for the Van Gogh.
