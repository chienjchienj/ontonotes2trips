benefit

1.1: help (or be helped by) someone or something

  • I have benefited greatly from her wisdom.
  • Your organization will benefit from a few of the many ways it pays to recycle.
  • How can we benefit those who need our help?
  • He says that the peace accord will benefit his country.
  • The research would benefit from an injection of new ideas.

1.2: financially fund

  • The series will benefit a Dance Scholarship Fund.
  • The party will benefit a dance center and a music school.
  • Your purchase will benefit a charity.
  • He asked for a small contribution to benefit a banquet fundraiser.
