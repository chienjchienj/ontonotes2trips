confuse

1.1: mistake one thing for another

  • You are confusing me with the other candidate.
  • Many people confuse money with happiness.
  • I confused your hair extension for a rat.

1.2: befuddle, disturb one's mental state

  • These questions confuse even the experts.
  • Too many tools will confuse the user.
  • The constant attention of the young man confused her.

1.3: jumble or make unclear

  • Has man once again confused the truth of God?
  • Bringing up conspiracy theories only confuses the issue.
  • She confuses the words when she tries to write a sentence.
