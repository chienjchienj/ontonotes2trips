scream

1.1: utter a loud, piercing cry or communicate in such a voice

  • Samantha screamed in pain.
  • The neighbors heard them screaming at each other for hours.
  • "I hate you!" she screamed.

1.2: create a loud, piercing sound

  • The planes screamed through the sky.
  • Brakes screamed as she swerved to avoid the debris.

1.3: strongly indicate a quality or identity

  • There was something in his demeanor that screamed desperation.
  • Her clothes screamed back office drone.
  • His plates said Iowa, but his accent screamed New York.
