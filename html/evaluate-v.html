evaluate

1.1: consider or examine to judge its value, quality, importance, or condition

  • We evaluated the situation carefully.
  • How do you evaluate this grant proposal?
  • It's impossible to evaluate these results without knowing more about the research methods.
  • We need to evaluate how the new material stands up to wear and tear.

1.2: estimate or calculate the numerical value of something

  • The appraiser evaluated the property at $100,000.
  • The damage has been preliminary evaluated at about one billion dollars.
  • Evaluate the following integral: 3x+2.
