pretend

1.1: behave unnaturally (with intent to deceive); act fictitiously

  • Should Hillary pretend to be a flight attendant?
  • I'm pretending to be a tiger! Rawr!
  • Bogus calls pretend to be the police.
  • Pretend (that) the world has ended.
  • Let's pretend!

1.2: put forward a claim and/or assert possession of; feign or act, usually with intent to deceive; play a role for personal gain.

  • Never pretend to a love which you do not feel.
  • He pretended the title of King.
  • Melody pretended the role of predator in The Black Forest.
  • I cannot pretend to know the truth.
