announce

1.1: make known, declare

  • She announced at dinner that she was getting a tatoo.
  • The President is announcing the cessation of hostilities on TV right now.

1.2: name, identify

  • He announced the winners of the spelling bee.
  • The butler will announce each guest as they arrive at the ball.

1.3: foretell

  • Flocks of migrating Canadian geese announce the coming of fall.
  • For some, the sinking of the Titanic announced the end of the Edwardian era.
