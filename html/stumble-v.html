stumble

1.1: trip; walk as if about to fall

  • She stumbled over the books on the floor.
  • As he stumbled about, he ran right into a tree.

1.2: encounter by chance

  • At last, he's stumbled onto something good.
  • I stumbled across some information you may find interesting.

1.3: make an error

  • The Japanese have watched the Europeans and Canadians stumble in the U.S. market.
  • They stumbled when asked how many siblings Kevin had, finally agreeing
  • on three brothers and three sisters.
  • Unfortunately, they stumbled by crafting a game that's too simple for strategy
  • gamers, and too convoluted for kids.
