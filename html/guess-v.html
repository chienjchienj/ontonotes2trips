guess

1.1: think, suppose, casually say

  • I guess I'll have the steak.
  • I guess she is angry at me for standing her up.
  • He guessed that he would do the same again.
  • Looking in the mirror at his sharp raccoon teeth, he guessed that he probably would eat meat.

1.2: predict, estimate, make a conclusion about an unknown

  • Jackie Chan said he guessed two of the Olympic mascots before they were unveiled.
  • Who could have guessed that she would grace the covers of major magazines?
  • Ever guessed who is about to call you?
  • Be sure to use a password that would not be easily guessed.
  • 23% guessed that the image was some kind of sea or ocean creature.
