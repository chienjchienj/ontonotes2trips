marry

1.1: take as a spouse; wed

  • Can you believe he married his high school sweetheart?
  • Hannah and Sean will marry in June.
  • She married into a fine old family.

1.2: cause to become married

  • The minister married them in a lengthy ceremony.
  • They were married by her cousin, who is a priest.
  • Her parents married her to the neighboring land owner.

1.3: become united

  • I let the stew simmer for 6 hours, because the flavors marry better that way.
  • I'm very interested in how different colors marry up with each other to create a striking web page.
