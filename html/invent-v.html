invent

1.1: create or devise something for the first time through thinking

  • In 2004, he invented a helmet made from coconut shell.
  • My grandmother invented the peanutbutter, bacon and mayonaise sandwich.
  • Galen invented the term 'gonorrhea' from the words for genital and flow.
  • We are inventing the organizations of the 21st century.
  • Prediction is hard, so instead I talked about inventing the future rather than predicting it.

1.2: make up something fictional or untrue

  • That's quite a story you've invented.
  • She invents a new personality and new interests for each new boyfriend.
