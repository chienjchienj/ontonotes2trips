age

1.1: grow or cause to grow old

  • The death of his wife caused him to age fast.
  • She aged gracefully.
  • Too much sun ages the skin.
  • 25 per cent of those aged 16 to 19 years were using cannabis.
  • (consider this example to be a reduced relative clause)

1.2: mature and improve over time

  • The wine is aged in oak barrels.
  • The steaks are prime, aged for flavor, grilled at 1800 degrees.
  • He ages cheese from his own factory as well as cheese produced by others.
