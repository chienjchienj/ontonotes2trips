opt

1.1: Select as an alternative over another.

  • She opted for the job on the East coast.
  • It remains to be seen how many managers will opt to retire.

1.2: OPT OUT: Select against an alternative.

  • I've opted out of my mutual fund.
  • She opted out of the joint venture with another company.
  • We opted out of going to the restaurant.

1.3: OPT IN:

  • He opted in to the settlement.
  • She opted into a pyramid scheme and lost thousands of dollars.
