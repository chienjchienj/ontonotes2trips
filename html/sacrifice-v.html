sacrifice

1.1: surrender, give for a specific purpose

  • He has sacrificed his job and his family's comfort in the struggle to uphold truth.
  • He sacrificed a bishop, two rooks and his queen to deliver checkmate.
  • I will never think of sacrificing her happiness to ambition.

1.2: sell at a loss

  • Storekeepers sacrificed their merchandise at half-price to unburden themselves.
  • He sacrificed his stock and paid off his debt.
