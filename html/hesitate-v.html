hesitate

1.1: hold back in uncertainty or unwillingness

  • Authorities hesitate to quote exact figures.
  • The FBI won't hesitate to act.
  • Do you hesitate to buy what you like?
  • Neighbors hesitated to call the police.

1.2: interrupt temporarily an activity

  • He hesitated before giving Slavin the knock-out blow.
  • As he pulled towards a green light, he hesitated, then decided to speed up.
