violate

1.1: transgress, infract, breach

  • They are violating the terms of that treaty.
  • The dictator violated every notion of human decency.

1.2: harm,despoil,desecrate

  • That darn cat has violated my herb garden again!
  • Vandals have been violating synagogues in that area.
  • Years of strip-mining have violated the landscape.

1.3: rape sexually

  • The plundering soldiers violated the village women.
