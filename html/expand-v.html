expand

1.1: extend, enlarge, increase or spread something

  • The dough will expand when you let it sit in a warm place.
  • He needed more capital to expand his business.
  • Hubble's Law supports the theory that the universe is expanding.
  • We'd like to expand our living room by extending the back of the house.
  • Many new jobs were created and ventures funded when the economy was expanding.
  • The charges against him were expanded to include conspiracy.
  • American culture has expanded around the world through movies and television.

1.2: amplify, elaborate or clarify a belief, statement or position

  • She expanded on the themes of free trade and globalization.
  • You need to expand your analysis in chapter two.
