recruit

1.1: (attempt to) convince to enroll, join or participate

  • The party recruited many new members.
  • One of our students was recruited by Google to California.
  • We have successfully recruited several new individuals for faculty positions.
  • Fortunately, a construction company recruited him to build tactical bases.

1.2: raise an army

  • The Officer complains of the difficulty in recruiting soldiers in this area.
  • Throughout the 1980s we recruited an Army of more than 750000 entirely from volunteers.
  • The Parliamentarians also raised money and recruited an army.
