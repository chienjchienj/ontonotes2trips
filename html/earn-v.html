earn

1.1: to receive money or payment for work done

  • She earns a lot in her new job.
  • This merger earned us lots of money.
  • How much do you earn in your new job?

1.2: to acquire or deserve something by one's efforts or actions

  • His speech earned him invitations to a number of lectures.
  • Student achievers earn praise from the community.
  • It's been a tough six months, and I feel I've earned a few weeks off.
