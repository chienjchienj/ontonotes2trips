cheer

1.1: cause to become happy

  • Maia cheered her daughter with a hot-fudge sundae.
  • School officials were cheered by the increase in applications.
  • Sunny days always cheer me up.

1.2: become happy

  • It's not that bad. Cheer up.
  • Cynthia cheered at the thought of a long, hot bath.

1.3: show approval or encouragement, esp. by shouting

  • Everyone cheered the birthday boy when he blew out the candles.
  • The crowd cheered the passing runners.
  • Shareholders cheered the acquisition of Acme Penwipes.
  • We all cheered on our favorite player.
