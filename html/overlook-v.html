overlook

1.1: fail to notice; ignore

  • How could she have overlooked all those typos?
  • He overlooks the fact that news is put together to appeal to viewers.
  • I'm going to overlook that last comment.

1.2: be oriented so as to look down on

  • My office overlooks the parking lot.

1.3: watch over; supervise

  • Mr. Casaubon does not like any one to overlook his work. --George Eliot, Middlemarch
