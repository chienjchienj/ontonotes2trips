laugh

1.1: produce laughter

  • I haven't laughed out loud at a comic in quite awhile.
  • I thought he was joking so I laughed loudly in his face.
  • Jenna blushed and laughed softly as she noticed that he was looking at her.

1.2: speak in a laughing manner

  • He laughed that at 102, he doesn't have many friends his own age.

1.3: deal with a problem by laughing or pretending to be amused

  • There's something about the eeriness of this episode that you can't laugh off.
  • But he laughed away their warnings.

1.4: LAUGH AT: ridicule

  • After all, the experts laughed at the Wright Brothers too!
