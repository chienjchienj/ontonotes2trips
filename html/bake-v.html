bake

1.1: cook and make edible by putting in a hot oven

  • Xavier baked a cake in the oven.
  • Julie baked muffins for the president.
  • Steve loves to bake in his kitchen.

1.2: heat by a natural force

  • The sun bakes the valley in the summer.
  • The heat of the humid, hazy afternoon baked the churchgoers.

1.3: be very hot, due to hot weather or exposure to the sun

  • The town was baking in the sun.
  • My fair, Irish skin will surely bake if I don't use SPF 90!
  • The churchgoers baked in the heat of the humid, hazy afternoon.
