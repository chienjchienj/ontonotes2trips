exist

1.1: be

  • What was there before the universe existed?
  • Mary's romantic interest in John existed only in his mind.
  • Crime simply does not exist in their small society.
  • This law exists to protect consumers against fraud.

1.2: subsist, survive

  • They existed on only nuts and wild berries for two weeks in the mountains.
  • They can't exist in New York City on a dollar a day!
  • Many people manage to exist without a cellphone or computer.
