bet

1.1: stake money or something of value on the outcome of an issue

  • I bet you $10 you can't lift that rock.
  • He regularly goes to the races and bets heavily.
  • I bet you my blue marble that I'll get there before you.
  • I'm betting on the new horse.

1.2: express certainty (or take action on the assumption) that something is true

  • I bet she's missed the bus.
  • I bet (you) that he won't come.
  • I bet he's forgotten to bring the keys.
  • You bet!
