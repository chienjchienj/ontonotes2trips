crush

1.1: compress out of shape or into small pieces

  • The car was crushed beyond recognition.
  • Crush the asprin into a powder and mix with some applesauce.
  • I've crushed my fingers in the door!
  • They crushed as many people into the stands as they could.

1.2: subdue, vanquish, obliterate

  • The government crushes anyone who dares to disagree.
  • The Bears crushed the Lions, 28 to 0.
  • We've come a long way in crushing racism.
  • His resignation crushed all our hopes of reform.
  • He crushed out the flames with his boot.

1.3: oppress, overwhelm

  • She was crushed by the death of her son.
  • If she says no to his invitation, it will crush him.
  • They were crushed by debt.
