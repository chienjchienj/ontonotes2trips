grasp

1.1: Physically hold firmly.

  • The baby grasped at its bottle.
  • It was hard to grasp my pencil after the accident.
  • He grasped his mother's hand.

1.2: Understand the meaning of something.

  • Do you grasp the meaning of this letter?
  • I can't grasp his newest novel.
  • She can't quite grasp why someone would have made the choices I've made.
