signal

1.1: communicate or make known by gestures, action, or signs

  • She signaled her impatience.
  • They have signaled their willingness to negotiate.
  • I signaled the driver to proceed.
  • Will you signal now?

1.2: indicate, be signs or symptoms of

  • The gauge indicates a temperature below freezing point.
  • These symptoms signal infection.
  • The economic indicators signal that the euro is undervalued.
