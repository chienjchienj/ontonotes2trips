list

1.1: enumerate or arrange items in a series; be included in such series

  • I can list on one hand the times he has returned my calls.
  • Let's see if we can list all the state capitals.
  • She listed the things she needed to do.
  • Glassware was listed on Mary's bridal registry at the store.
  • She would like to be listed among the prominent socialites of Washington.
  • He's listed among the founding members.
  • She lists herself as a club member but never attends meetings.

1.2: (cause to) physically tilt, lean over

  • The boat seems to be listing to port.
  • The Titanic listed badly before finally sinking.
  • Erosion listed the old tree.
