characterize

1.1: describe someone or something; assign attributes to

  • You can characterize his behavior as that of an egotist.
  • Was the employer justified in characterizing her as an independant contractor?
  • I am a little surprised to read that she characterized the job as customer service.

1.2: be representative of someone or something

  • What characterizes a Venetian painting?
  • Hearing actually characterizes our relationship more than seeing or even feeling.
  • Patience and good humour characterized the voting.
