depend

1.1: be contingent on

  • Interest rates will depend on whether the economy continues to heat up.
  • A vintage year for Cabernet grapes depends on many variables.
  • We'll hold the wedding ceremony under the awnings, or out in the garden, depending on weather.
  • Your grade will depend upon how well you do on the final exam.

1.2: count on or trust

  • Can I depend on you to watch the baby?
  • It will be cold in January, you can depend on that!
  • John says we can't depend on the main sail to not tear during a storm.
