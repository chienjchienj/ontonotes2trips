evolve

1.1: undergo evolutionary development

  • Did birds evolve from dinosaurs?
  • Humans did not evolve from monkeys.
  • Over time, they evolved into species whose legs held their bodies off the ground.

1.2: develop, work out

  • Can Enterprise 2.0 evolve from Enterprise 1.0?
  • How has he evolved as a businessman and as a public figure?
  • The bill has evolved to a House-Senate compromise.
  • She evolved a lot musically as she got older.
  • Ten years ago we said we would evolve our programs and services.
