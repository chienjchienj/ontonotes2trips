want

1.1: desire, seek

  • He wants to marry her.
  • The company doesn't want another scandal.
  • I want you all to be quiet!
  • My neighbor was wanted for questioning by the police.

1.2: require or need something that is missing

  • This garden wants the hand of a good gardener.
  • His parents were determined that he should want for nothing.
  • The refugees are still wanting shelter although food has been provided.
