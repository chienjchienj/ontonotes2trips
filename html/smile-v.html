smile

1.1: change facial expression by spreading the lips, often to express pleasure

  • He thought about his friends back in the forest and for a moment he smiled.
  • She smiled that cold, enigmatic smile again, and my blood turned to ice.

1.2: communicate a message by or while smiling

  • Silently he poured her a drink, and when he handed it to her, she smiled her thanks.
  • But yes, this time the terms cancelled and he smiled his assent.
