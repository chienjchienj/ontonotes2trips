taste

1.1: Have a distinctive or characteristic taste, perceptible by an experiencer.

  • Can you taste the garlic?
  • So many apples taste mealy and bland.
  • This tastes of nutmeg.

1.2: Take a sample of; try; distinguish flavors.

  • Taste these new crackers!
  • We tasted wines last night.
  • She paused to taste the Georgian shashlik.

1.3: Experience briefly.

  • The ex-slave tasted freedom shortly before she died.
  • I want to taste true love.
