freeze

1.1: Change to ice or ice-like form, be cold, anesthetize

  • The water in the bowl froze.
  • Freeze the leftover food.
  • Water freezes at 32 degrees.
  • Your doctor may use liquid nitrogen to freeze the wart.
  • I could freeze in this office with the air conditioning on.

1.2: Stop dead, suspend

  • When he saw the police car he froze.
  • US House votes to freeze aid to Vietnam.
  • Freeze the assets of this hostile government.
  • She froze when she saw her ex-husband.
